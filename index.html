Welcome to Akan name identifier

Through the Akan name identifier, you will be able to know your akan name.

Akan names are derived from Ghanian culture. Frequently in Ghana, children are given their first name as a 'day name' which corresponds to the day in the week they were born. Here are some of Ghanian day names;

Kwasi, Kwadwo, Kwabena, Kwaku, Yaw, Kofi, Kwame

If you want to know your Akan name all you have to do is two simple steps

  1. Enter your birth year
  2. Enter your gender
